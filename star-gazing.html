Stargazing

Lectures last approximately 30 minutes and if the weather permits (see chart below), they are followed by 90 minutes of rooftop star-gazing through our many telescopes. We also offer astrophotography slideshows (small, informal lectures) and Q&A with astronomers during the event so that people can cycle between the various stations (or get a respite from the cold on the roof). Attendance varies from 50-300 people depending on the weather and the topic. Lectures, slideshows, and star-gazing are aimed at the layperson, but children will get something out of them too.
